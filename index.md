# Privacy Policy for MuslimOnePro

**Last Updated:** January 4, 2026

## Introduction

MuslimOnePro ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our Islamic education and prayer companion application.

---

## Information We Collect

### Location Data (Optional)
- **Purpose:** To calculate accurate prayer times (Fajr, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, Isha) and Qibla direction
- **Type:** GPS coordinates (latitude/longitude)
- **Storage:** Stored locally on your device only
- **Sharing:** Never shared with third parties or transmitted to our servers
- **User Control:** Device Settings → MuslimOnePro → Location

---

### Notification Access (Optional)
- **Purpose:**
  - Prayer time notifications (Adhan alerts)
  - Azkar (daily remembrance) reminders
  - Fasting reminders
  - Islamic calendar events
- **Storage:** Stored locally on your device only
- **Sharing:** Never shared
- **User Control:** Device Settings → MuslimOnePro → Notifications

---

### Local Data Storage
Stored strictly on your device:
- Prayer history & streaks
- Quran bookmarks
- Hadith favorites
- Reading progress
- Language preference (English/Arabic)
- Reminder settings
- Adhan audio preferences
- Location cache

No cloud sync is used.

---

## Third-Party Services

### Aladhan Prayer Time API
- **Purpose:** Backup prayer time calculation when offline GPS is unavailable
- **Data Sent:** City name or coordinates
- **Stored by Us:** ❌ No
- **Privacy Policy:** https://aladhan.com/privacy-policy

---

### Quran.com API (Development Only)
- Used only one time during development to fetch translation data
- Not used during runtime

---

### Local Prayer Calculation
- **Library:** `adhan` by Batoul Apps
- **Method:** ISNA
- **Processing:** 100% offline on device

---

## Data We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect:
- Names, emails, phone numbers
- User accounts or logins
- Device identifiers
- Analytics or tracking
- Advertising identifiers
- Payments or financial data
- Contacts or photos
- Any cloud-stored personal information

---

## How Your Data Is Used
All data is processed locally on your device:
- Location → Prayer times & Qibla only
- Notifications → Islamic reminders only
- Local storage → Personal app experience only

---

## Data Security
- All data is stored locally using secure device storage
- No authentication system exists
- No servers store user data
- Data is deleted automatically when app is removed

---

## Children's Privacy
